Chartpati Shivaji Maharaj....

( शिवाजी शहाजी भोसले )

१९ फेब्रुवारी, १६३० - ३ एप्रिल १६८०

The Great warrior king of India.

Shivaji Maharaj is the founder of Maratha dynasty and a warrior king of Maharashtra. He is known as Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was born in the Shivneri Fort in Maharashtra on 19 February 1630 to mother Jijabai and father Shahaji Rage bhosale. It is said that mother Jijabai to goddess Shivai on Shivneri Fort for a brave son and kept her son name as Shivaji. At 15 years, he conquered the Torna Fort. The Chakan fort and the Kondana Fort were taken by bribing the Adil Shahi governor. Afzal Khan tried to attempt treachery with Raje Shivaji, Shivaji killed him with Tiger claws. He became the biggest enemy of Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb. He was arrested in Agra Fort by Aurangzeb, but he escaped by using great wits.

Rajmudra of Shivaji Maharaj.

प्रतिपच्चंद्रलेखेव वर्धिष्णुर्विश्ववन्दिता। शाहसुनो शिवस्यैषा मुद्रा भद्राय राजते॥

( crescent-moon, the kingdom of Shivaji, son of Shahaji, will always seek the welfare of the people.)
